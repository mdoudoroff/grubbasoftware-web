Grubba Software Announces TrueGrain 2

Raleigh, North Carolina—March 15, 2017—Grubba Software announces the immediate availability of TrueGrain 2, a completely new version of their venerable black & white film simulation software for Macintosh and Windows. The new product is a ground-up rewrite with a new user interface, dramatically improved performance, more refined output, and additional features.

Highlights of TrueGrain 2 include:

  • New user interface with Retina/high-DPI display support
  • Memory allocation limitations of TrueGrain 1 are eliminated
  • Much faster in every regard
  • All grain samples improved with more even exposure across the frame
  • Five films added that were not available for TrueGrain 1 (total available film stocks 21)
  • Film download manager (easier installation)
  • More manual control over grain expression

TrueGrain 2 is a black and white film simulator for digital photographers that can accurately reinterpret a color digital image into an image virtually indistinguishable from a scanned black and white film original of the same scene. Images processed with TrueGrain 2 incorporate the actual film grain sampled from a specific film stock and exhibit that film’s typical exposure characteristics. In comparison with other software tools that rely on parametric noise generators and improvised curves to synthesize (mimic) different film looks, TrueGrain 2 rigorously applies a scientific approach of sampling and profiling. TrueGrain 2 is a uniquely accurate and literal tool for recapturing the aesthetics of black and white photography.

TrueGrain 2 is available immediately for US$89. Half-price upgrades for TrueGrain 1 customers are also available.

More information, samples, and a downloadable demo are available at https://grubbasoftware.com.
